Clinical Named Entity Recognition & Diagnosis Similarity Analysis
CISC 488: Introduction to NLP (December 2025) · Python · BERT · CRF · HuggingFace · Word2Vec · scikit-learn · k-means · cosine similarity

Clinical NLP + similarity modeling to simulate case-based diagnostic support from unstructured notes.

  • Built a clinical NLP pipeline to extract medical entities and identify diagnostically similar patient cases from unstructured clinical text.
  • Implemented and compared CRF-based vs. BERT-based NER models using precision/recall/F1 across disease, symptom, drug, and test entities.
  • Created a synthetic BIO-tagged clinical dataset (75 snippets, 1,000+ tokens) to simulate real-world EHR access constraints.
  • Generated embeddings with Word2Vec and BERT; applied cosine similarity + k-means clustering to group related clinical cases.
  • Added interpretability visuals (e.g., t-SNE/UMAP, heatmaps) to understand model output and cluster structure.
